About Me

My Journey

I am a passionate learner of software engineering who believes in continuous growth and curiosity. I started exploring technology to solve real-life challenges and to build meaningful solutions that make everyday life better. Though I began my coding journey later than some, I firmly believe it’s never too late to learn something new and excel at it.

Goals in This Program

  • Strengthen my foundation in front-end and back-end development.
  • Develop confidence in building and deploying complete web projects.
  • Collaborate with peers to enhance problem-solving and teamwork skills.
  • Transition into a professional software engineering role after completion.

Areas of Low Confidence

I sometimes struggle with debugging complex issues and optimizing performance. However, I’m working to improve these areas by taking on more challenging coding tasks and practicing consistency in project reviews and refactoring.

Note to My Future Self

Remember why you started this journey — not just to code, but to create. Keep learning, stay humble, and don’t let setbacks discourage you. Progress takes time, but the persistence you show now will define your success later.

Extra Thoughts

I’ve realized that community and collaboration play a huge role in becoming a better developer. I hope to contribute to open-source projects soon and inspire others who are just starting out.
